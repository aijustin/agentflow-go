# Code of Conduct

This project aims to be a practical, respectful place to build agent infrastructure.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and constructive.
- Discuss ideas and code, not personal attributes.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and do not publish secrets, private logs, or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
